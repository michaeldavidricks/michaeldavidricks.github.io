Welcome! I am an applied public economist working as an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln and a Resaerch affiliate of the Youth Policy Lab. My research applies theory and econometrics to analyze the welfare implications of government programs, including education policy, energy, and other public services.

Prior to coming to the University of Nebraska, I received my PhD from the University of Michigan and spent the 2022–2023 school year as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the National Bureau of Economic Research. Before to graduate school I studied economics at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah.
