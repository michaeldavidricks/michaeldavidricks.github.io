Welcome! I am an applied public economist working as an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Nebraska - Lincoln. My research applies theory and econometrics to analyze the welfare implications of government programs, including education, energy, and other public goods.

I received my PhD from the University of Michigan, spent the 2022-2023 school year as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the National Bureau of Economic Research. Before that I did my undergraduate degree at Brigham Young University in Utah.
